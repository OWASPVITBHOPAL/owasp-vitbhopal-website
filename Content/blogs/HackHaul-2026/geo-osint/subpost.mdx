# GeoOsint Challenge Writeup

**Category:** OSINT  
**Points:** 250  
**Author:** Saurabh Sharma

## Challenge Description
"We received this suspicious photo from an unknown source. Can you tell us where this person was standing?"

**Attached Image:** `view.jpg`

## Step 1: Analyzing the Image
The image showed a distinct bridge in the background, a specific type of street lamp, and a blurry sign.

### Key Details
- **Bridge:** Cable-stayed bridge design.
- **Vegetation:** Palm trees suggesting a tropical or subtropical climate.
- **Text:** Partially visible text on a nearby shop.

## Step 2: Refining the Search
Using reverse image search engines, we focused on the bridge. The design closely matched the **Bandra-Worli Sea Link** in Mumbai, India. However, the angle was slightly different.

Trying another reverse search on the street lamp led us to a few possible locations in Southeast Asia.

Finally, checking the text "Cafe..." led us to search for cafes near famous bridges.

## Step 3: Pinpointing the Location
After narrowing it down via Google Maps and Street View, we found the exact spot. It was indeed a location in **Goa**, near the Mandovi River bridge (Atal Setu).

Wait, upon closer inspection of the bridge's pylon structure in the high-res version, it was identified as the **Signature Bridge** in New Delhi.

But the real answer was hidden in the EXIF data all along! just kidding. The EXIF was stripped.

Re-evaluating the "Cafe..." sign, it read "Dilli Haat".

**Location:** Near Signature Bridge, Wazirabad, data-center Delhi.

## Final Coordinates
We submitted the coordinates of the viewing deck.

`28.7460° N, 77.2285° E`
